# Prioritized Quarterly Roadmap

## Q1 2026: MVP Development

*   **Goal:** Develop the core functionality of the MVP.
*   **Owner:** Lead Developer
*   **Estimates:**
    *   **Development:** 10 weeks
    *   **Testing:** 2 weeks
*   **Key Deliverables:**
    *   Secure user authentication and authorization.
    *   Core AI agent functionality.
    *   Basic business education content ingestion.

## Q2 2026: MVP Launch

*   **Goal:** Launch the MVP to a limited number of pilot users.
*   **Owner:** Project Manager
*   **Estimates:**
    *   **Development:** 8 weeks
    *   **Testing:** 2 weeks
    *   **Launch:** 2 weeks
*   **Key Deliverables:**
    *   Simple web interface.
    *   FERPA-compliant data handling.
    *   Logging and monitoring.
    *   MVP launch to a limited number of pilot users.

## Q3 2026: Final Product Development

*   **Goal:** Develop the advanced features of the final product.
*   **Owner:** Lead Developer
*   **Estimates:**
    *   **Development:** 12 weeks
    *   **Testing:** 2 weeks
*   **Key Deliverables:**
    *   Advanced AI agent capabilities.
    *   Integration with other educational platforms.
    *   Comprehensive curriculum of business education content.

## Q4 2026: Final Product Launch

*   **Goal:** Launch the final product to the general public.
*   **Owner:** Project Manager
*   **Estimates:**
    *   **Development:** 10 weeks
    *   **Testing:** 2 weeks
    *   **Launch:** 2 weeks
*   **Key Deliverables:**
    *   Sophisticated web interface.
    *   Advanced security features.
    *   Final product launch to the general public.
